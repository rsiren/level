Playbook

Design rationale

Built on your 24-test profile. Two targets: the execution gap (Self-Discipline 9/20 vs Intellect 20/20) and the hidden low-mood/stress pattern (Depression facet 13/20, PSS 25/56, emotional awareness 7/20). Everything is a self-experiment — your autonomy and internal locus of control reject imposed regimes.

The daily structure
  • Evening = planning organ. Tomorrow's commitment + if-then plan is set after work, when you have energy. Implementation intentions: d = 0.65 across 94 studies (Gollwitzer & Sheeran 2006). Decisions made the night before survive low-willpower mornings because they're already made.
  • Morning = 30-second pulse only. Mood + energy on the phone. No decisions required — by design. If mood ≤ 2, one sentence of affect labeling (Lieberman 2007: naming dampens amygdala reactivity; your emotional awareness is 7/20 so this is targeted training).
  • Evening review = accountability. Did the committed thing happen? Execution rate counts truth, streak counts showing up.
The suggestion engine
  • Low mood → behavioral activation (SMD −0.74, equal to CBT; Ekers 2014) + one reappraisal rep (your ERQ reappraisal is mid-range and trainable).
  • Low energy → movement first (exercise rivals antidepressants for mild low mood).
  • Execution tactics rotate daily — your Openness kills fixed routines.
  • Framing: stakes and metrics, not virtue and cheerleading — low Agreeableness, high Achievement.
Weekend deep session

WOOP/MCII (doubled follow-through in RCTs, Oettingen), weekly review, 2 mastery + 2 pleasure blocks (behavioral activation targets your SWLS 21/35 vs Flourishing 47/56 gap), PSS-4 stress pulse tracked over time.

One-time setup (5 min)
  1. On github.com: Settings → Developer settings → Personal access tokens → Fine-grained tokens → Generate. Account permissions → Gists: Read and write. Nothing else. Long expiry.
  2. Paste the token above → Create database. This makes a private Gist and fills in the Gist ID.
  3. On the iPhone: open this same page, paste the same token and Gist ID → Save & connect. Then Share → Add to Home Screen.

The token is stored only in this browser's localStorage on your devices, scoped to Gists only.
